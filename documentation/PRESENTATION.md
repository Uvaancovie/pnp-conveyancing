## üöÄ What the Application Does

### For Clients (Self-Service Tools)

#### 1. **Transfer Cost Calculator**
- Instant calculation of transfer duty, attorney fees, and deeds office costs
- Fully compliant with South African transfer duty brackets
- Based on actual purchase price of property
- **Client Benefit**: Transparent cost visibility before commitment

#### 2. **Bond Cost Calculator**
- Calculates bond registration fees and attorney costs
- Includes all disbursements (FICA, deeds searches, rates clearance)
- Supports bonds from R50,000 to R20,000,000+
- **Client Benefit**: Complete financing cost understanding

#### 3. **Bond Repayment Calculator**
- Monthly repayment estimates based on interest rate
- Shows total interest paid over loan term
- Helps clients understand affordability
- **Client Benefit**: Financial planning clarity

#### 4. **WhatsApp Lead Generation**
- Direct "Start My Transfer" button
- Pre-populates client details into WhatsApp message
- Routes to **071 187 1327** (your business number)
- **Client Benefit**: Instant connection to your team

#### 5. **User Profiles & Saved Calculations**
- Clients can create accounts (Email/Password authentication)
- Save calculations for future reference
- Track property transaction journey
- **Client Benefit**: Personalized experience and record-keeping

### For Pather & Pather (Business Intelligence)

#### 6. **Admin Dashboard** (Restricted Access)
Comprehensive analytics platform with three powerful views:

- Total property value calculated

- Calculation type distribution (Transfer vs Bond vs Repayment)
- Recent activity feed (last 10 client actions)

- Complete user directory
